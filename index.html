How It Works

Create a Creative and Unique Product ID


Distinctive and non-predictable product ID is of utmost importance. Combination of letters, numbers and timestamp ensures avoidance of conflict with existing IDs. Most importantly, avoid to use sensitive data within product ID to maintain privacy and security.

Connet Your Product ID with Blockchain


Public blockchain offer robust security feature, including immutability, where data once added to the chain cannot be altered or deleted. The transparent recording of data ensures its public accessibility. Additionally, leveraging digital signature allows customers to verify product authenticity and trust in the manufacturing process from a reputable company.

Verifiable Ownership


NFTs grant verifiable ownership of digital assets, and when applied to physical products by blockchain connected companies, the same feature can be extended. Each product ID uniquely links to a single registered customer, enabling effective product tracking and eliminating duplicates with the same ID, fostering enhanced market integrity.
